First Appearance

Python was conceived in the late 1980s,[28] and its implementation began in December 1989[29] by Guido van Rossum at Centrum Wiskunde & Informatica (CWI) in the Netherlands as a successor to the ABC language (itself inspired by SETL)[30] capable of exception handling and interfacing with the operating system Amoeba.
